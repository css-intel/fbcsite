Why We Give

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." - 2 Corinthians 9:7

Giving is an act of worship and obedience to God. Your generosity enables us to spread the Gospel, care for our community, support missions worldwide, and maintain a vibrant church family. Every gift, no matter the size, makes a difference in advancing God's kingdom.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is online giving secure?
Yes! We use industry-leading encryption and security measures to protect your information. Your payment details are processed through a secure, PCI-compliant payment gateway.
Will I receive a tax receipt?
Yes! You will receive an email confirmation immediately after giving, and we will provide a comprehensive tax statement each January for all your contributions during the previous year.
Can I give to a specific ministry or fund?
Absolutely! When you give online, you can designate your gift for general offering, missions, building fund, benevolence, or other specific purposes. Use the "Special Instructions" field to provide additional detail.
How do I set up recurring giving?
Simply select "Weekly" or "Monthly" in the frequency dropdown when completing your online giving form. You can modify or cancel recurring gifts at any time by contacting our church office.
What if I need help with online giving?
We're here to help! Contact our church office at (803) 555-1234 or email info@friendshipbaptist.org, and our staff will gladly assist you with setting up online giving or answering any questions.
