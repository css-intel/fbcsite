Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love others, and serve the world.

At Friendship Baptist Church, we are committed to being a community where people can encounter the transforming love of Jesus Christ. We strive to create an environment where individuals and families can grow in their faith, develop meaningful relationships, and discover their God-given purpose.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our ultimate authority for faith and practice.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is the Son of God, born of a virgin, died for our sins, rose from the dead, and is coming again.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone, not by works.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and mission.

Baptism & Communion

We practice believer's baptism by immersion and regularly observe the Lord's Supper as ordinances of the church.

Our History

1895

Foundation

Friendship Baptist Church was founded by a small group of believers committed to serving the Aiken community. Meeting initially in a small wooden building, the congregation grew steadily through faithful witness and community service.

1920s

Growth & Expansion

As the church grew, a larger sanctuary was built to accommodate the expanding congregation. Sunday School classes and youth programs were established, creating a vibrant church community.

1965

Community Impact

The church launched its first community outreach programs, including a food pantry and clothing ministry that continue to serve hundreds of families each year.

1990s

Modern Era

A new worship center and education building were constructed, providing state-of-the-art facilities for worship, children's ministry, and community events.

2025

Today

With over 130 years of faithful ministry, Friendship Baptist Church continues to be a beacon of hope in Aiken, SC. We remain committed to our founding mission while embracing new opportunities to reach our community with the Gospel.

Our Vision for the Future

We envision Friendship Baptist Church as a thriving, multigenerational community where every person discovers their purpose, experiences genuine fellowship, and grows in their relationship with Jesus Christ.
