A Word from Pastor James Anderson

Dear Friends,

Welcome to Friendship Baptist Church! For over 125 years, our church has been a beacon of hope and love in the Aiken community. We are a family of believers committed to growing in faith, serving one another, and reaching out to our neighbors with the good news of Jesus Christ.

Whether you're searching for a church home, seeking answers to life's big questions, or simply looking for a community where you belong, we invite you to join us. At Friendship Baptist, you'll find genuine fellowship, biblical teaching, and opportunities to make a difference in the lives of others.

We would be honored to worship with you this Sunday!

In Christ's Love,
Pastor James Anderson
