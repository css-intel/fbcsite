Directions & Parking

Address

Friendship Baptist Church
123 Church Street
Aiken, SC 29801

Parking

We have ample free parking available in our main lot off Church Street and an overflow lot accessible from Oak Avenue. Handicap parking spaces are located near all entrances.

From I-20

Take Exit 18 for Highway 19/Whiskey Road. Turn south and follow for 4 miles. Turn right on Richland Avenue, then left on Church Street. The church will be on your right.

Accessibility

Our facility is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps and elevators to all floors. Assisted listening devices are available at the sound booth.
