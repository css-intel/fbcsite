Children's Ministry

Nurturing young hearts with the love of Jesus

Ages: Birth - 5th Grade
When: Sundays 9:00 AM & 10:30 AM
Where: Children's Building
Contact: Sarah Mitchell

Our Children's Ministry provides a safe, fun, and engaging environment where kids can learn about God's love through age-appropriate lessons, worship, games, and crafts. We believe every child matters to God and to our church family.

Programs Include:

  • Nursery (Birth-2): Safe, loving care with trained volunteers
  • Preschool (3-5): Bible stories, songs, and hands-on activities
  • Elementary (K-5th): Dynamic lessons, worship, and small groups
  • Vacation Bible School: Exciting summer program with missions focus
  • Kids Choir: Musical training and performance opportunities

Youth Ministry

Empowering the next generation to live boldly for Christ

Ages: 6th - 12th Grade
When: Sundays 6:00 PM, Wednesdays 7:00 PM
Where: Youth Center
Contact: David Chen

Our Youth Ministry creates a space where teenagers can ask tough questions, build authentic friendships, and develop a faith that's their own. We're committed to walking alongside teens as they navigate the challenges of adolescence with biblical truth and grace.

What We Do:

  • Sunday Night Worship: Relevant teaching, worship, and community
  • Wednesday Small Groups: Deeper discussions and accountability
  • Summer Camp: Week-long retreat for spiritual growth and fun
  • Mission Trips: Hands-on service opportunities locally and globally
  • Monthly Events: Game nights, service projects, and social activities

Missions & Outreach

Serving locally and reaching globally with Christ's love

Contact: Robert Williams

We believe the Gospel compels us to action - caring for the poor, sharing good news, and demonstrating God's love through practical service. Our outreach ministries provide multiple opportunities to make a tangible difference.

Local Outreach:

  • Food Pantry: Serving 100+ families monthly with groceries and essentials
  • Clothes Closet: Free clothing for those in need
  • Community Meals: Hot meals served every Tuesday evening
  • Prison Ministry: Sharing hope with incarcerated individuals
  • Hospital Visitation: Comfort and prayer for the sick

Global Missions:

  • Missionary Support: Partnering with 15 missionary families worldwide
  • Mission Trips: Annual trips to Mexico, Haiti, and Asia
  • Compassion International: Child sponsorship program
  • Disaster Relief: Rapid response to natural disasters

Bible Study & Small Groups

Growing deeper in God's Word together

When: Sundays 9:00 AM, Wednesdays 7:00 PM
Contact: Rev. Michael Thompson

Spiritual growth happens best in community. Our Bible studies and small groups provide intimate settings where you can ask questions, share life, and apply Scripture to everyday challenges.

Sunday School Classes:

  • New Believers: Foundations of Christian faith
  • Young Adults (18-30): Relevant biblical teaching for life stage
  • Adults (30-50): Book-by-book Bible study
  • Senior Adults (50+): Life application and fellowship
  • Women's Class: Topics relevant to women's spiritual journey
  • Men's Class: Biblical manhood and discipleship

Midweek Groups:

  • Home Groups: Neighborhood-based fellowship and study
  • Women's Bible Study: In-depth study with childcare provided
  • Men's Accountability: Early morning groups for men
